Who I Am

Hi, I'm Tracy Mendoza, a UX/UI Designer. As a seasoned sales executive, I utilize my client relations skills to get to the heart of understanding user's problems in order to create the best resolutions to meet their goals. I welcome opportunities to collaborate with an innovative organization that places client user experience at the forefront of its mission.

My spare time is usually spent trying out new sewing projects, taking an evening walk with my dog, or exploring the Bay Area (aka finding the best food spots!).
